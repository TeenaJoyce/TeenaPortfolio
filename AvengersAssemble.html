Avengers Assemble

Software used: 3ds Max

A meticulously designed 3D environment representing the Avengers' boardroom. Through the strategic placement of objects, each symbolizes a distinct Avenger, contributing to a visually engaging and dynamic representation of the superhero team's iconic headquarters. This project highlights my creative and design skills, offering a unique and immersive glimpse into the world of the Avengers.
