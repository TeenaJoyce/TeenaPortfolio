AVENGERS ASSEMBLE

Software used: 3DS Max

In a digital creation inspired by the Avengers, a 3D model of their meeting room comes to life. It's a sleek space filled with iconic objects representing each hero. Captain America's shield rests proudly in a glass case, while Thor's hammer rests in the center beconing anyone to try to lift it. Iron Man's suit stand on stand-buy, Hawkeyes quiver bag and black windows gauntlet are just chilling in the lounge area. All the obkect in the scene were created from 2d and 3d simple objects and were brough to life with bitmaps and materials
