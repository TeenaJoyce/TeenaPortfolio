Luna Crisp Packaging

Software used: Adobe photoshop and Illustrator

A solo project on product packaging. Took to designing a crisp packet.

After researching different crisp packaging, I created my own combination of flavors using major ingredients from Chinese cuisine - soy sauce and chili. The logo "Luna" was designed to highlight the connection to the moon, suggesting a sense of mystery, elegance, and a nighttime snacking experience.
