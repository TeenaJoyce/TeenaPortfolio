After researching different crisp packaging, I created my own combination of flavors using major ingredients from Chinese cuisine. The logo "Luna" was designed to highlight the connection to the moon, suggesting a sense of mystery, elegance, and a nighttime snacking experience.

Story

Naming:The idea was to create crips with chinese flavours and as we all know the moon is an important concept in chinese culture- yin/yan, lunar new year, the full moon etc. Hence the name "luna" came to be.

Typography:The font chosen is "Le Havre Rounded". It was selected based on th look and how I want the customers to feel. The logo does not need a sleek typeface but an elegant one and the rounded, cursive look of the font conveys a sense of elegance and softnes(not too harsh or sharp) to make it feel inviting.

Color Palette:The color palette differes for each flavour except for the black gradient it starts with. The colors used represent the main ingrediants used for the flavor.
