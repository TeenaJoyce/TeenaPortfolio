Rumble in the Jungle

Software used: Audacity and reaper

An innovative audio project that transforms the concept of a children's storybook into a musical poem about jungle animals. Me and my team recorded live audio of each animal, carefully editing and seamlessly embedding them into the final audio track. This project showcases my creativity in merging storytelling, music, and sound design, resulting in an engaging and immersive experience for young audiences.
