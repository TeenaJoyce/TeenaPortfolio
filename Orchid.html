Orchid perfume

Software used: Adobe Illustrator and Canva

An individual project taken to create a new brand design. I wanted to explore the idea of creating my own brand. Hence I designed a logo and an ad with the color palette and the fonts chosen to represent "calmness and harmony". Perfumes have a way of creating a connection between scents and emotions allowing fragrances to influence our mood in various ways. I wanted my perfume brand to entice peace and calmness
