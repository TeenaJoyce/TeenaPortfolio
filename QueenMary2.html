Queen Mary Cruise Liner- Swimming pool and Ballroom

Software used: 3ds Max and Blender

Final Group project- a 3d unity game that utilizes sounds and jump scares to creat mystery and tension.

we recreated a 3D model of the Queen Mary's cruise liner, and I designed the 1st class ballroom and the swimming pool. Both were modeled as close to the real cruise liner as possible.
