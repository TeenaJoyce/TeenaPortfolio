Queen Mary Cruise Liner- Room B340

Software used: 3ds Max and Blender

In a digital creation inspired RMS Queen Mary's cruise liner. A 3d model of the 1st class swimming pool and ballroom came to life. The scene was modeled with inspiration taken from the cruise liner. It was develeoped for a horror game that takes place inside a cruise ship.
