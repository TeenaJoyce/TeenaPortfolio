Queen Mary Cruise Liner- Room B340

Software used: 3ds Max and Blender

Final Group project- a 3d unity game that utilizes sounds and jump scares to creat mystery and tension.

In this project we recreated a 3d model of the Quee marys cruise liner and I was assigned to create the infamous Room B340- a room rumored to be haunted till this day.It is a basic room with 4 sections- the bedroom , bathroom, sitting room and a closet room. The pallete used is to represent isolation.
