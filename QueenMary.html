Queen Mary Cruise Liner- Room B340

Software used: 3ds Max and Blender

In a digital creation inspired RMS Queen Mary's cruise liner. A 3d model of the aleged haunted room B340 came to life. The scene was modeled with the actual room as inspiration. It was develeoped for a horror game that takes place inside a cruise ship. The color and and materiels were chosen to give an eerie feeling when all the lights were turned off since the game takes place at night.
